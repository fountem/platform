## The Pitch
Political misinformation in the UK is not a content problem. It is an infrastructure problem. The content exists — Full Fact publishes 750 fact-checks a year; the IFS and ONS produce rigorous independent analysis. The infrastructure to connect that content to the people who need it, at the speed they need it, does not exist. Fountem is building that infrastructure.

## Why Luminate
- Information integrity: every Fountem claim links to primary source. Methodology published before product launches.
- Civic participation: designed for the voter who is not currently using civic information tools. Postcode-first, plain-English, mobile-first.
- Luminate has backed AWO (data rights), Lighthouse Reports (open newsroom), Forbidden Stories (collaborative journalism) at this early stage.
