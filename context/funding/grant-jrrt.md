## Project Title
Fountem: Evidence-Based Political Intelligence for Low-Propensity Voters in the UK

## The Problem
The voters most exposed to political misinformation — Reform UK's core support (34% among households earning under £20,000/year) — are the least well-served by existing tools. Full Fact, TheyWorkForYou, WhoCanIVoteFor are trusted by Guardian readers, not the voters who most need them.

## Why JRRT Fits
Three stated priorities addressed directly:
1. Increasing participation of low-propensity voters — platform designed explicitly for mobile-first, plain-English use by non-engaged voters
2. Improving quality of democratic information — root cause layer shows what evidence says is causing problems voters care about
3. Reducing influence of misinformation — Unfaked bot directly addresses false political claims on social media

## Deliverables
Month 3: Live platform covering all May 2026 council elections. Postcode lookup, candidate profiles, party positions, evidence panels, methodology page.
Month 6: Post-election impact report. Unfaked bot live with documented accuracy benchmarks. Donor transparency layer.
Month 12: Full year operation data. Academic evaluation partnership TBC. B2B revenue stream initiated.
