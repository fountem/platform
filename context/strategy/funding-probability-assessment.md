## Application 1: Innovate UK Frontier AI Phase 1 (£105k-175k)
**10-15% chance.** Frontier AI competition is looking for step-change improvements in model architecture. Fountem's RAG pipeline is applied AI, not frontier AI. Needs a university research partner (City Journalism School, Oxford Internet Institute) as co-applicant to strengthen odds materially.

## Application 2: Nuffield Foundation (£180k)
**5-10% chance.** Eligibility problem — Nuffield funds researchers with publication records at universities. Solo technical founder does not match profile. Becomes 25-35% if a named academic researcher from Oxford Internet Institute or Reuters Institute is listed as PI with Fountem as delivery partner.

## Application 3: JRRT UK Democracy Fund (£75k) — PRIORITY
**30-40% chance.** Best fit. Rolling applications. Funds technology projects. Time-pressure argument (elections) works well. Incorporate immediately (Companies House, £12, 24 hours), submit within 2 weeks, get Democracy Club letter of support.

## Application 4: Luminate (£250k)
**5% near-term.** Luminate does not accept unsolicited applications. Process-driven funder. Goal of sending pitch NOW is to be on radar for September 2026 when Fountem has working product + users. The pitch document is an introduction, not an application.
