## Revenue Streams (sequential)

### Stream 1: B2B API Licensing (Month 6-12)
Target buyers: newsrooms, political research firms, brand safety platforms.

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|---|---|---|
| Newsroom Starter | £500/mo | 10k API calls/month |
| Newsroom Pro | £1,500/mo | 50k API calls, real-time, custom alerts |
| Enterprise | £5,000+/mo | Unlimited, white-label, SLA |

Target: 5 Starter customers = £2,500/mo. 2 Pro = £3,000/mo. Realistic by Month 9-12.

### Stream 2: Grant Funding (bridge)
Expected value across 4 applications: £53k-74k. JRRT at 30-40% is most likely.

### Stream 3: Consumer Premium (Year 2+)
£4.99/month or £39/year. At 1,000 paying subscribers: £60k ARR. Upside, not primary model.
