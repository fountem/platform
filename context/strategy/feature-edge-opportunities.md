# Feature Edge Opportunities — Fountem

## Top 3 Feature Bets
1. **Evidence Graph** — strongest moat, best trust story. Every conclusion traceable to a navigable evidence chain. Proprietary structure, not just content.
2. **Correction Packs** — best distribution edge, fastest to ship. Purpose-built share objects: X reply, WhatsApp card, screenshot card. Makes accurate info as easy to share as misinformation.
3. **Claim-to-Policy Monitoring** — best strategic bridge between Unfaked and Fountem. Turns isolated claims into monitored stream connected to policy debate.

## Feature Tiers

### Tier 1 — Build Now (0-6 weeks)
- Correction Packs (2-4 weeks, high distribution leverage)
- What Would Change This Verdict? (1-2 weeks, cheapest trust feature in the stack)
- Party Trust Dossiers (2-3 months party-level)
- Evidence Graph (3-6 months strong first version)
- Claim-to-Policy Monitoring (2-4 months narrow first version)

### Tier 2 — Build Next (2-4 months)
- Narrative Tracker
- Verification Workspace (lightweight — B2B lever)
- Local Reality Layer (pilot first)

### Tier 3 — Build Later (6-12 months)
- Provenance + Verification Layer
- Citizen Question Capture
- Candidate Trust Dossiers (local)

### Tier 4 — Do Not Build Yet
- Full Viral Prediction (expensive, data-hungry, operationally risky early)
- Identity Intelligence (legal risk outweighs value)
- Full Partner Collaboration Suite (6-month distraction)
